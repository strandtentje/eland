Rules

Scoring points and winning

Teams are ranked according to the points that they earn, the team with the most points wins. In case of a tie the amount of points gained by drinking beer will be used as the tie breaker. The team who drank more beers will win in this case.

You can score points in two different ways. The first is drinking beer, the other is finishing problems successfully. For each finished beer you will receive 1 point. Finishing problems will score you a number of points depending on whether your team finished the problem first, second, third, etc. The points gained are as follows:

First 10 points
Second 8 points
Third 7 points
Fourth 6 points
Fifth or later 5 points

When to drink

To keep things fun for everyone drinking is entirely voluntary. We expect you to honour the following rules however:

  • Take a drink when your code fails to compile.
  • Take a drink when your program crashes.
  • Take a drink when your program doesn't produce the correct output.
  • Take a drink when a team finishes a problem before you do.

Stuck?

Have you ran into a problem for which you can't think of a solution? Don't fret, another problem will come along shortly. We aim introduce a new problem every 30 minutes. When a team finishes a problem early we will introduce a new problem about 10 minutes later. Or after the regular 30 minute timer, whichever comes first. In the mean time you can earn some extra points by drinking beer!
